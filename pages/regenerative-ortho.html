What is Regenerative Orthopedics?

Regenerative Orthopedics is a revolutionary approach to healing musculoskeletal injuries and conditions by harnessing your body's natural ability to repair itself. Unlike traditional treatments that focus on managing symptoms regenerative medicine aims to restore damaged tissues to their original form and function.

At Align Plus Ortho, we specialize in Orthobiologics – the use of biological substances such as platelet-rich plasma (PRP) to stimulate and enhance the body's natural healing mechanisms.

Our treatments are minimally invasive, promote faster recovery, and can often help patients avoid surgery or delay the need for joint replacement procedures.

Our Regenerative Treatments

Advanced biological therapies for optimal healing and recovery

Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP)

Concentrated platelets from your own blood, rich in growth factors that accelerate healing of tendons, ligaments, muscles, and joints.

Stem Cell Therapy

Using your body's own stem cells to regenerate damaged tissues, reduce inflammation, and promote healing in cartilage, bone, and soft tissues.

Bone Marrow Aspirate Concentrate (BMAC)

Concentrated bone marrow containing mesenchymal stem cells and growth factors for enhanced tissue regeneration.
