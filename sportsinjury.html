Prevention & Treatment Strategies

Comprehensive approach to sports injury management

Biomechanical Analysis

Advanced gait and movement analysis to identify and correct improper techniques that lead to injuries.

Strength & Conditioning

Sport-specific training programs to build strength, improve flexibility, and prevent overuse injuries.

Proper Equipment Assessment

Evaluation of sports gear, footwear, and protective equipment to ensure optimal fit and function.

Training Load Management

Monitoring and optimizing training intensity, duration, and recovery periods to prevent overtraining.

Nutrition & Hydration

Dietary guidance to support performance, recovery, and injury prevention.

Mental Preparation

Psychological strategies to enhance focus, reduce anxiety, and prevent performance-related injuries.

Regenerative Therapies

PRP, stem cell treatments, and other biologics to accelerate healing and tissue repair.

Advanced Arthroscopy

Minimally invasive keyhole surgery for ligament repairs, cartilage restoration, and joint cleaning.

Comprehensive Rehabilitation

Sport-specific rehab programs to restore function, strength, and return to play safely.

Ultrasound-Guided Injections

Precise delivery of medications, corticosteroids, or biologics to injured tissues.

Shockwave Therapy

Non-invasive treatment for chronic tendon injuries and calcifications.

Return to Sport Protocol

Structured, phased approach to safely return athletes to their sport at optimal performance levels.

Common Sports Injuries We Treat

Expert care for a wide range of athletic injuries

ACL & Knee Injuries

ACL tears, meniscus injuries, patellar tendinitis, and runner's knee

Shoulder Injuries

Rotator cuff tears, shoulder dislocations, labral tears, and impingement

Ankle & Foot Injuries

Sprains, Achilles tendinitis, plantar fasciitis, and stress fractures

Back & Spine Injuries

Herniated discs, spondylolysis, sciatica, and muscle strains

Elbow & Wrist Injuries

Tennis elbow, golfer's elbow, wrist sprains, and TFCC tears

Hip & Groin Injuries

Hip labral tears, groin strains, hip impingement, and bursitis

Muscle Strains

Hamstring, quadriceps, calf, and adductor muscle tears

Fractures & Dislocations

Stress fractures, acute fractures, and joint dislocations
